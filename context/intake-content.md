## 3. Services

### Daycare
Our daycare program is built around structured play and purposeful enrichment, not just open-group activity. Dogs are thoughtfully placed into playgroups or guided through private play and enrichment sessions, with constant supervision and access to both indoor and outdoor spaces. Every day is designed to balance socialization, stimulation, and rest.

### Boarding (Dogs and Cats)
Boarding at Boxers is an extension of our daily programming, ensuring consistency, comfort, and care around the clock. Pets enjoy clean, secure accommodations with attentive overnight supervision, along with the option to participate in daycare or enrichment activities during their stay. Every visit is designed to feel familiar, safe, and structured.

### Grooming
Our grooming services are tailored to keep your dog clean, comfortable, and well-maintained. From baths and nail trims to full-service grooming, each session is handled with patience and attention to your pet's individual needs.

### Enrichment & Private Play (BEC)
Our Boxers Enrichment Center (BEC) offers a more personalized approach for dogs who benefit from focused attention or alternative play styles. Through private play sessions and guided enrichment, we create meaningful engagement tailored to each dog's energy level, personality, and comfort. It's a premium, individualized experience within our all-inclusive philosophy.

### Additional Services
We offer a range of complementary services and add-ons to enhance your dog's experience, from enrichment upgrades to retail products that support their health and happiness. Every offering is designed to align with our commitment to thoughtful, high-quality care.
